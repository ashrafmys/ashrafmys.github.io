Neurogenesis – Keep Your Brain Growing

Tue 07 February 2017

This is talk by neuroscientist @ Ted : You grow new brain cell and how to do it

Key points from the talk :

1- Your brain still grows neuron even you are already an adult

2- There are certain factors that can give positive and negative impact on neurogenesis

3- Factors that can enhance neurogenesis :

-Exercise, especially running or cardiovascular activity

-Learning

-Good sleep

-Intermittent fasting

-Calorie restriction

-Flavonoids like dark chocolate

-Blueberries

-Caffein

-Omega 3 fatty acids

-Curcurmin

-Zinc

-Resveratrol

-Folic acids

4- The following will inhibit neurogenesis :

-Sleep deprivation

-Sedentary lifestyle/lack of exercise

-High saturated fat and high sugar diet

-Defficiency of vitamin B/A/E – Alcohol
