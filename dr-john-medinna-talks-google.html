Dr John Medinna Talks Google

The notes are based on his talk. Watch his video here

These points are basically from his book Brain Rules. He only talked a few of them in this video.

Some of the key points from the video :

1- The brain is designed to solve problem related to surviving, in unstable outdoor environment and to do so in constant motion

2- Exercise boosts brain power.

Aerobic exercise can significantly improve executive function of the brain. Weight lifting however doesn’t seem to improve this function.

Both aerobic & weight lifting doesn’t enhance the memory function.

Exercise specifically aerobic exercise can reduce cognitive pathology (depression, dimentia, etc)

How much exercise to get cognitive improvement? 2 – 3 times a week of 20 minutes aerobic exercise

3- Stressed brains don’t learn the same way as unstressed brain. Stress can ‘damage’ the brain. The more stress you feel, the less productive you’ll be.

Relax and learn
