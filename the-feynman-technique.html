The Feynman Technique

Thu 19 January 2017 by ashrafm

Who is Richard Feynmann ?

A famous physicist who won Nobel prize. He was really a funny guy. You should read or listen to his book – You Must Be Joking Mr Feynman. Great book !

The Feynman Technique

1. Find a topic. Study it.

2. Explain the topic either by writing it on paper or verbal explanation to your friends using your own words.

For the best result, write and talk about the topic. If you have no one to explain to, talk to yourself while imagining you are teaching someone.

3. Simplify the explanation If you truly understand the subject, you will have no problem to explain it in simpler way.

Cut the words. Use analogy. Use drawings. Explain to your audience like they are kids.
