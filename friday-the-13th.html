Friday the 13th? Coincidence?

Remember this date – Friday 13th Jan 2017. This was the day this site was borned into the cyber world.

It seems to me that at the end of the day, good ol’ WordPress was the one that I can rely upon, despite all the bloats that WordPress is associated nowadays.
