Rejection - Your Emotion Workout

Tue 07 February 2017

Rejection is emotionally painful. It can lead to all sort of negative feelings – sad, angry, disappointment, embarrassment.

Unfortunately, these feelings will influence your action, your attitude, your response to the world around you.

If you allow yourself to respond negatively to these emotions, you will always be in a losing position. Your emotion won’t get stronger.

In order to change this, you need to continuously expose yourself to the rejections and at the same time you control how you respond to the situations.

You’ll train your emotion to adapt to the rejections. Over time, you will be calmer, more relaxed, less temper when facing the rejections.

After a while, rejection won’t intimidate you anymore. You’ll feel ‘mehhh’ and you move on. At this point, you will be unstoppable. No rejection can change your emotion.

Those with muscular body or incredible strength don’t simply get big or strong overnight. It takes time and lots of exercise.

The same goes with your emotion. Get rejected often. Control how your respond. Strengthen your emotion.

Soon, you will be unstoppable.
